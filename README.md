## skills-repo
A curated, opinionated library of Claude skills for engineers, product managers, and makers who ship things.

Each skill is a self-contained folder — drop it into your Claude setup and get best-practice reasoning, structured outputs, and real-world templates for every stage of the product lifecycle: from writing PRDs and ADRs to reviewing code, running incidents, planning roadmaps, and communicating with stakeholders.

No fluff. Every skill ships with a quality bar baked in.
